What is False Ceilings?

A false ceiling is a fitted ceiling that hangs below the original ceiling of a room or home. It’s usually suspended by metal frames being versatile enough to be constructed in homes of any size, giving your homes a stylish look.

The perfect ceiling design varies for each room and each home and depending on the available space. The surrounding walls and the overall theme of the house, there are infinite patterns and colors to choose from giving your home a stylish & customized Look.
